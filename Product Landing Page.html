Japanese Harmony

"Japanese Harmony Whisky" refers to "Hibiki Japanese Harmony," a blended Japanese whisky produced by Suntory, known for its smooth, balanced taste achieved by combining malt whiskies from the Yamazaki and Hakushu distilleries with grain whisky from Chita, all aged in various casks including American white oak, sherry, and the rare Japanese Mizunara oak, giving it complex flavor notes like honey, citrus, and sandalwood; the name "Harmony" reflects the intention to create a well-integrated blend that embodies the subtleties of Japanese nature and craftsmanship.

Blossom Harmony

Blossom Harmony is a limited edition Japanese whisky that celebrates cherry blossoms, or "sakura," and Japanese culture. It's a blend of malt and grain whiskies from Suntory's Yamazaki, Hakushu, and Chita distilleries, finished in Sakura Wood Casks.

Yamazaki Single Malt

Yamazaki Mizunara Single Malt is comprised of meticulously selected malt whiskies all aged for a minimum of 18 years in solely Mizunara casks, created in honor of Suntory Whisky's centenary.
